4. LAUNCH: Select "Try or Install" to enter the live desktop.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. TROUBLESHOOTING: "HELP, IT WON'T BOOT!"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If your computer boots straight back into Windows/Mac, check these two things:

A. DISABLE SECURE BOOT (Most Common Issue)
   Many modern PCs block "unsigned" software like Linux. 
   1. Enter your BIOS/Setup (Usually tap F2, Del, or Esc during startup).
   2. Find the "Security" or "Boot" tab.
   3. Change "Secure Boot" to [Disabled].
   4. Save and Exit. Try booting your NIXKEY again.

B. BITLOCKER (Windows Users)
   If you see a "BitLocker Recovery" screen, don't worry! Your files are safe. 
   Simply remove the NIXKEY and reboot into Windows to get your Recovery Key, 
   or just use the NIXKEY for "Live" mode only without touching your hard drive.
